About us

YPS AI

This tool was developed by Yahya Qanie, with support from Lena Slachmuijlder and Saji Prelis of Search for Common Ground, and with technical development led by Murtaza Qanie. It was created to make information on the Youth, Peace and Security agenda — anchored in UN Security Council Resolution 2250 — more accessible and to strengthen the YPS movement among organizations, academics, and policymakers.

What sources it uses

This tool is grounded in a curated knowledge base that includes publicly available UN resolutions and frameworks, UN publications, regional organization documents, national action plans and strategies, academic research, and publications from civil society and non-governmental organizations.
